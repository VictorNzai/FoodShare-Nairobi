About FoodShare Kenya

FoodShare Kenya is a community-powered initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger in Nairobi. We connect generous food donors with verified charities, ensuring surplus food reaches those who need it most.

Our Mission: To create a sustainable, transparent, and impactful food-sharing ecosystem for all.
Our Vision: A Nairobi where no one goes hungry and no food goes to waste.
Our Values: Community, Transparency, Impact, and Sustainability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate food?

Anyone with surplus food—restaurants, supermarkets, event organizers, or individuals—can donate through our platform.

How do you ensure food safety?

We work only with verified charities and provide clear guidelines for safe food handling and transportation. All donations are tracked for accountability.

Is there a cost to use FoodShare Kenya?

No, our platform is completely free for both donors and charities. We believe in making food sharing accessible to all.

How can I get involved?

You can register as a donor or charity, volunteer with us, or help spread the word. Every action counts!

How do I contact FoodShare Kenya?

Visit our Contact page or email us at info@foodsharekenya.org.
